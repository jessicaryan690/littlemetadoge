Little Baby Doge is a hyper-deflationary decentralized buyback token, community-driven project that aims to reduce global warming by being the no 1 cryptocurrency platform for fighting climate change!

Founded in Stockholm, Sweden in 2021 by an international team of climate change activists who wanted to find a way to take action against the dangerous effects of global warming. Little Baby Doge is created with one purpose - to help make the world a better place. One of our core values is “one for all, and all for one” which means that we are committed to giving back in every way possible. For each transaction made on LBD, 1% will be direct to Greta Thunberg and her Foundation. We believe that being part of the solution starts by taking responsibility for ourselves and our actions and supporting those who do it best – like Greta!

Buyback

The buyback function is a major part of the LBD’s strategy. It lowers supply and raises demand, which results in an immediate effect on price when activated.

Burn

The tokens bought through buyback are immediately burned. This creates a true burn and guarantees the price per token will increase every time a buyback is activated because each activation reduces supply, which increases demand for what's left of the current circulation.

Locked Liquidty

We want to make sure that all of our investors are protected, which is why we're locking Little Baby Doge LP Tokens. For the sake of security and trust in our LBD community!

Fight climate change with Little Baby Doge

Little Baby Doge is a team of anonymous climate change activists, who are all about making the world a better place for future generations. The main objective of Little Baby Doge is to help promote environmental awareness through engaging content and innovative projects that will foster change in our society.

Many scientists are predicting that the global temperature will exceed 1.5 degrees Celsius within five years as a result of climate change, which has been at risk for spiraling out of control if not handled right away. The Little Baby Doge community is very much focused on fighting this issue and we have partnered with many initiatives to ensure sustainability in an eco-friendly environment where people can live more sustainably without harming our planet any further than it already has been harmed by humankind's own actions!
